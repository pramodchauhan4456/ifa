2005

Mr. Abhishek Goenka founded IFA Global in this year & the company started operating from Kolkata with a vision of having its presence all over the country

2007

The corporate office was moved to Mumbai the financial capital of the country to provide personalized services to our existing clients.

2008

"Momentous year" as we expanded our clientele base to 100+ & became a trusted & sought after name in the field of FX advisory

2009

Its views were covered extensively by leading financial dailies and business news channels.

2010

Reached a twin milestone of serving more than 200+ companies and employee strength of 50+ at our Mumbai office

2012

IFA was referred "The most consistent views on FX markets" by CNBC Awaaz. It also launched a report in association with FIEO at Taj Mahal Palace titled: "Rupee may hit 60 by June 2013"

2013

The 2012 report was covered by major publications like Economic Times & Hindu Business line, Rupee did touch 60 against the US Dollar in June 2013

2014

IFA completed 10 years of existence & inaugurated 3 of its regional offices in Bangalore, Ahmedabad & New Delhi to provide personalized services to its clients

2015

IFA went international as it opened operations in Dubai to cater to Middle East & African clients

2016

IFA Global introduced "Treasury Elite"; a non-profit forum conceptualized exclusively for CEOs CFOs & Treasury & finance professionals to facilitate knowledge sharing, mentoring & networking

2017

- IFA introduced for the first time in India a dedicated "FX Managed Account Desk" for HNI's & Corporates
- Equipped with state of art infrastructure to accommodate our growing human capital, IFA took possession of a New office at Kanakia Zillion BKC Annexe

2018

Launch of "IFA Global Treasury Research Academy" (IFA-TRA), for market research, case studies & certification programmes.

Abhishek Goenka

Founder & CEO

Mr. Abhishek Goenka is a young entrepreneur who started at the age of 24 and is spearheading one of the prominent and trusted companies in Treasury Consulting and FX portfolio Management space – IFA GLOBAL over the last 13 years. He holds a bachelor’s degree in commerce and a MBA in Finance, and has undertaken various certified executive programs from Harvard University. Under his leadership, the team manages portfolio and risk management for over 200 companies having a cumulative exposure of over 20 billion USD.


He is well sought for his FX views by business channels like Bloomberg, CNBC, ETNOW, ZEE, Forbes, Economic Times etc. He actively contributes to the financial stability report of RBI. He is also a frequent speaker on FX and treasury markets across most of the industry associations and many prominent MBA institutes in the country. He is the chairman of Treasury Elite - one of the most prominent forums for entrepreneurs, CFO’s and Treasurers in India. He is actively involved in various initiatives for the upliftment of women and children through IFA Global Foundation. He is also the chief mentor for IFA Global Treasury Research Academy.

A voracious reader, passionate about fitness and human psychology and spirituality (Vipassana). He stays in Mumbai with his wife and 2 daughters.
