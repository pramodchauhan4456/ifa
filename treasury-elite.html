Who we are

Treasury Elite events present an ideal opportunity to its participants to network, interact & discuss the best practices being followed in treasury management. The TE events are conducted thrice in a year. This event is by invitation only and only the TE members are a part of it.

Mission

To become a well-connected network of skilled and influential Treasury Professionals and Managing Directors for knowledge sharing, networking and career growth

Who should attend

If you are a CEO, Chairman, Fund Managers, CFO, Treasurer, Chief Risk Officer, FX Heads or involved in any aspect of the Financial Markets and Treasury Risk Management
